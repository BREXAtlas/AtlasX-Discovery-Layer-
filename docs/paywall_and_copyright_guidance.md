# Paywall and Copyright Guidance

AtlasX can process user-provided documents locally. Users are responsible for
lawful access.

## Public Repositories

- Do not commit copyrighted PDFs.
- Do not commit licensed full text unless you have clear permission.
- Keep private source folders ignored by Git.
- Commit citation metadata, schemas, prompts, code, and fictional examples.

## Extracted Notes

Extracted notes should preserve citation and DOI. They should not replace the
original source or misrepresent the author. When in doubt, store only metadata,
short notes, and source traces that let a lawful reader verify the claim.

## Licensed Sources

Follow your institution's license terms. Some licenses may allow personal or
institutional analysis while prohibiting public redistribution.
