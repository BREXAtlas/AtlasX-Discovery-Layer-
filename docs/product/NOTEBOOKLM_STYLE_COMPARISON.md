# Notebook-Style Comparison

This document is for internal and product positioning. AtlasX Discovery Notebook
is not affiliated with Google and should not use Google branding as part of its
product name.

## Similar Category

AtlasX belongs to the category of AI notebooks over user-provided sources. It
can help users ask questions, extract structure, and generate outputs grounded
in local source material.

## Differences

AtlasX is designed to be:

- Local-first.
- Open-source.
- Inspectable.
- Extensible.
- Ontology-aware.
- Discovery-focused.
- CLI-first today and app-wrapped later.

Product language should say `notebook-style`, `source-grounded`, or
`local-first discovery notebook`. It should not say `Google clone`.
