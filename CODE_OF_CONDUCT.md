# Code of Conduct

AtlasX welcomes constructive participation from researchers, students,
publishers, developers, and organizations.

## Our standards

- Be respectful and specific in critique.
- Attribute ideas, sources, and prior work fairly.
- Avoid harassment, personal attacks, and discriminatory language.
- Make room for domain experts, early-career researchers, and users from
  institutions with different levels of access.

## Research integrity

Because this project touches research interpretation, contributors should avoid
overclaiming, misattribution, and unsupported conclusions. AtlasX outputs are
research-support artifacts and require human expert review.

## Reporting concerns

Please report conduct concerns to the project maintainers through the security
or maintainer contact listed in the repository. Maintainers may remove content,
pause discussions, or restrict participation when needed to protect contributors.
